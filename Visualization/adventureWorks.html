About the Project
Using the Adventure Works dataset available as 7 CSV flat files, a visualization was created for a monthly report for stake holders using Power BI Desktop. This included importing the data, creating the data model and DAX measures to calculate the orders, revenue and return related metrics, and creating the report
This report is split into 3 sections overall
  1. Executive Summary
  2. Product Detail
  3. Customer Detail
Executive Summary : The Summary shows the monthly Revenue, Orders and Returns, Orders split by category name, sub category name, product name. It shows the overall top product by the total orders and revenue. It additioanlly shows the distrubuting of sales by the continent

Product Detail : This shows the performance of the product on the 3 metrics - Orders, Revenue and Returns against its monthly targets. The user can input a Price Adjustment % and visulize the impact on the profit. There is also a forecast for the weekly performance and the weekly history of the return volume for the product

Customer Detail : Gives bussiness a summary of the clients of the organization split by Gender, Income Level, Occupation, Age for the top 100 customers. It also shows the customer bringing in the maximum revenue for the company
