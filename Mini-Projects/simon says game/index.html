Simon Clicks
A new take on the Simon says game for you to play from your device. What you need to do is mimic Simon's actions.
At the start of the game, Simon will click a button. If you mimic him correctly, Simon will click yet another button. Now, you need to replicate all of his Clicks starting from the very first click & it has to be in the very same order!
Seems easy? Try getting a score of 15 and let me know!
